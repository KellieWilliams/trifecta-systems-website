Meet the Owner

The vision and passion behind Trifecta.Systems.

Kellie Williams, Owner

Kellie Williams brings a wealth of experience in technology, engineering, and business operations to Trifecta.Systems LLC. With a passion for empowering small businesses and nonprofits, she leverages her diverse skillset to help them thrive in today's digital landscape.

Extensive Background in Technology and Engineering:

  • Over 20 years of experience in various engineering and technology roles, from R&D to cybersecurity.
  • Proven ability to manage complex projects, troubleshoot technical issues, and implement data-driven solutions.
  • Experience in utilizing lean manufacturing principles to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Entrepreneurial Spirit and Focus on Small Businesses and Nonprofits:

  • Founded Trifecta.Systems LLC in March 2019, focusing on providing critical digital services to small businesses and nonprofits in her local area.
  • Deep understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities faced by small businesses and nonprofits in the digital age.

Education and Professional Development:

  • Holds a Bachelor of Science degree in IT Cybersecurity from Colorado State University (Cum Laude).
  • Prior Bachelor of Science degree in Materials Science and Engineering from California State Polytechnic University-Pomona.

Kellie's commitment to continuous learning and her passion for technology ensure that Trifecta.Systems LLC stays at the forefront of the industry, providing innovative solutions to help small businesses and nonprofits succeed.

What Kellie enjoys most about working with small businesses and nonprofits is the opportunity to build personal relationships with their owners and teams. She finds great satisfaction in seeing their businesses thrive and watching them achieve their goals.
